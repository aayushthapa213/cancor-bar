About Us

Established in 1990, 'Cancor' has been a staple of Lamjung for over 30 years. With a warm and welcoming atmosphere, we've become a favorite local spot for drinks, good food, and great company. Our commitment to using the freshest ingredients and providing excellent service has kept customers coming back time and time again. Whether you're looking to catch up with friends, enjoy a night out, or just relax with a cold drink, Cancor is the perfect place for you. Visit us today and experience a piece of Lamjung's history!
